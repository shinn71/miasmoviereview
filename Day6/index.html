If you don't know which movies to watch during long summer vacations, keep scrolling your screen just a little bit and find out the right one which is the most attracted to your soul!

1. Anne with an E

An orphan girl with some freckles on cheeks and red hair, whose name is Anne (Ann with an E), is brought to Avonlea which is a small farm village on Prince Adward Island. This is a 3-season series in which we will see how Anne strives hard so that she could adapt to everyone in the village, and that Matthew and Marilla, 2 old brother-sister duo, give her a warm place in green gables.

Genre: Period drama, Coming-of-age

Watch here for free!

2. Reply 1988

Reply 1988 is a series set around 1980, which gives off a bittersweet feeling to the audience. This South Korean television series is about 5 friends and their families with different financial conditions but with the same spirit, living along in a small ward, Ssangmun-dong. The casts' brilliant acting give the audience a sense of freedom, joy and relatable family situations, and finally make everyone feel nostalgic from the very corner of the heart.

Genre: Family, Rom-com, Coming-of-age

Watch here for free!

3. My Liberation Notes

A drama which highlights life of an ordinary office worker, and how she faces daily problems at workspace with the help of a strange man who lives the next door. Audience can see how these 2 people are dependent to each other,and how they heal the wounds in the heart of each other, in 16 slow-paced episodes. Besides, the series highlights how people who live far from downtown have to face some kinds of discrimination from their collegues.

Genre: Romance, Melodrama, Slice of life

Watch here for free!

4. Hospital Playlist

This is one of the most popular series, which shadows the stories of doctors, nurses and patients. Some people may think this is boring as the series is not about a love story, not about thriller, but I am sure your heart will experience pause and pound, and even feel warm as you see the most realistic side of a hospital. The scenes between 5 doctors will also make everyone realize how lovely a friendship can be.

Genre: Comedy, Medical Drama, Slice of life

Watch here for free!

5. Hometown Cha Cha Cha

A dentist in Seoul moves to a small village called Gongjin. At first, the environment between neighbours and her was a bit strange, as she looked down on them. After meeting a man called Hong, the thoughts of the dentist on the village eventually change, and she even starts to have a good feeling for Hong,but she tries to stop her feelings for Hong as she is a dentist while he is only an ordinary villager working as a fisherman. On the other hand, she could sense Hong cannot be just a fisherman. Is he a secret billionire who is hiding all of his wealthness, or is he really an ordinary man? What kind of ending would happen between them?

Genre: Romance, Healing, Country Drama

Watch here for free!
